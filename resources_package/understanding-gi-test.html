Understanding GI Tests and Procedures

Common Tests

Here are the most common tests you may undergo in our clinic:

  • Colonoscopy: Examines the colon to screen for cancer, polyps, and other issues.
  • Endoscopy: Examines the esophagus, stomach, and duodenum to diagnose ulcers, reflux, and more.
  • Capsule Endoscopy: A non-invasive way to examine the small intestine using a swallowable capsule with a camera.

What to Expect

During these procedures, you may be sedated to ensure comfort. The procedure itself may take anywhere from 15 minutes to an hour. Afterward, youâ€™ll be monitored until the sedation wears off.

After the Test
